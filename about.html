About Us

Vegas Vegan Van is a plant-based, fast-casual food truck specializing in innovative burgers, sandwiches and desserts. Vegas Vegan Van is more than just the newest iteration of the plant-based fast-casual experience, it is a brand new concept that will alter the public’s perception of plant-based restaurants.

Vegas Vegan Van is owned and operated by Eric Anderson and Kevin Cain. Together they have over 10 years of experience managing restaurants along with 5 years of experience in plant-based establishments. Eric and Kevin are energetic, fast-paced workers who excel in the kitchen, interacting with guests, and in managing the operational and financial details of a restaurant.

Vegas Vegan Van has been born of both an amazing opportunity for success and rapid positive change for animals, the environment and human health. Vegas Vegan Van is not just a single restaurant concept, it is designed from the ground up to be a chain of restaurants with the flexibility to open anywhere and operate seamlessly with very little support from a corporate office. The initial goal of Vegas Vegan Van is to bring great, accessible plant-based food to cities across the Southwest as well as larger untapped markets. Currently, Vegas Vegan Van has three locations: Las Vegas, Summerlin, and Henderson.
